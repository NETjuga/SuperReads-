About Chapterly

Project Overview

Chapterly is a modern all in one book/library application designed to enhance your reading experience and build up a reading habit for new readers. Built with a focus on simplicity and functionality, it helps readers organize and maintain their literary journey, every other app similar to this either SUCKS or has a stupid paywall for stupid features. My app is extremely simple to understand, utilise and benefit from since i added features that are actually practical..

Key Features

  • Personal Library Management
  • Reading Progress Tracking
  • Notes for insights on different books
  • Reading Statistics and Insights
  • Book Recommendations

Technology Stack

Built using Swift for iOS, with a clean and minimalist interface that prioritizes user experience. The app integrates with Apple's ecosystem for seamless functionality.

Future Development

Upcoming features include social sharing, reading challenges, integration with popular e-reader platforms, and a social network on the app to drive reading competition/consistency in a duolingo type of way
