About StudentWire

Project Overview

StudentWire is a career guidance platform that helps students discover their ideal career path through personality assessment and data-driven recommendations, and when the user upgrades to a premium version they get access to a dashboard that gives helpful information about industries and jobs.

Free Features

  • Big Five persionality assessment
  • IQ Assessment
  • Friend Compare, compare your results with your friends.
  • The users Top Career match

Paid Features

  • ALL job matches from HIGHEST to LOWEST, you might get a 99.7% match with Software Engineering (thats what i got) and 44% match with cloud engineering and Journalism (i highly dislike cloud engineering and journalism).
  • Industry insights | Get Salary Trends, Industry investment trends (From 2019-Present), Job Growth Trends and forecasting. (with detailed graphs and sources).
  • Salary Calculator | Get the average salary for your career path, based on your country, experience and job title.
  • Industry Match | When you talk about your skills in a certain industry, our AI will match you with the best career path for you within that industry.
  • Career Sim | Get a realistic idea of what the daily life of a certain career looks like, with a realistic salary, and a realistic job title.
  • Opportunity Finder | Find the best opportunities for you, based on your skills, experience, and location, get the opportunity based on what you've decided.
  • Step in | Get the detailed steps of how YOU can get your foot in the door of your dream career, based on realistic data.
  • SW GPT | Get the StudentWires GPT, to answer ANY question you have about your career, or any career related question and specifically based on the your results.

Technology Stack

Built using Javascript for the Frontend, node.js/Express for the backend and MongoDB for the database.
